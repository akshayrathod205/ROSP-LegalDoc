Labour Law

Labor law in India governs the rights and responsibilities of workers and employers, covering aspects such as wages, working conditions, industrial disputes, and social security. It aims to protect and regulate the labor force while promoting fair labor practices and equitable treatment of employees.

Consumer Law

Consumer law in India is a legal framework designed to protect the rights and interests of consumers, ensuring fair practices by businesses and providing mechanisms for consumers to seek redressal for product or service-related grievances. It encompasses various acts and regulations, including the Consumer Protection Act, aimed at promoting consumer welfare and holding businesses accountable for their actions.
